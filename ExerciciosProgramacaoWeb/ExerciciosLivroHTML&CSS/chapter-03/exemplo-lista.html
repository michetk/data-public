Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs crean

Method

  1. Melt butter in a fryng pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and ream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (ir will conrinue to cook on the plate until served)
